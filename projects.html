My Projects

Project 1: NOT Gate Circuit

I was responsible for designing and building the NOT gate circuit. I ensured that the inverter circuit functioned correctly, meaning the output was always the opposite of the input. I carefully connected the transistor and resistor to achieve proper inversion and tested the circuit to confirm accurate results.

Project 2: Hour of Code – AI & Game Workshop

This project was a collaborative Hour of Code activity where our group visited a local school to introduce students to programming and modern artificial intelligence concepts. We demonstrated game mechanics, AI logic, and helped students interact with beginner-friendly coding platforms.

We guided students step-by-step, showed real code execution, answered their questions, and helped them design basic smart behaviour in games. The goal was to inspire them to explore Computer Science deeply and understand how AI influences future careers. The outcome was highly successful and motivated the students to continue learning.

Project 3: Robot Construction and Assembly

I played an important role in the hardware aspect of the project by assisting in the construction and assembly of the robot. I helped ensure that the physical components were properly connected and fully functional. Additionally, I collaborated on the design and development of the marker holder, improving its durability and alignment so the robot could draw smoothly without interruptions.
