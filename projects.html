Project 2: Hour of Code – AI & Game Workshop

This project was a collaborative Hour of Code activity where our group visited a local school to introduce students to programming and modern artificial intelligence concepts. We demonstrated game mechanics, AI logic, and helped students interact with beginner-friendly coding platforms.

We guided students step-by-step, showed real code execution, answered their questions, and helped them design basic smart behaviour in games. The goal was to inspire them to explore Computer Science deeply and understand how AI influences future careers. The outcome was highly successful and motivated the students to continue learning.
