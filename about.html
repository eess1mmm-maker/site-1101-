About Me

I am a student enrolled in SITE 1101: Principles of Information Systems. This course has introduced me to the basics of web development, version control using Git, and hosting websites on GitHub Pages.

I was born in 2007 in Mingachevir, Azerbaijan. From a very young age, I developed a strong passion for mathematics. Numbers, logic, and problem-solving have always attracted me more than anything else, and this interest eventually pushed me to participate in multiple math competitions and academic events. One of my notable achievements was earning 2nd place in a regional mathematics festival called “Riyaziyyat elmlərin şahıdır”. This competition strengthened my confidence, expanded my analytical mindset, and motivated me to continue improving my academic potential. Over the years, mathematics has not only shaped my academic direction but has also helped me build discipline, patience, and strategic thinking. I enjoy solving complex problems, learning new mathematical methods, and exploring how mathematics connects to real-world systems such as programming, engineering, and data science. Right now, I am focused on improving my skills, deepening my mathematical knowledge, and preparing myself for future academic challenges.

I enjoy learning how technology can be used to build practical and user-friendly solutions.
